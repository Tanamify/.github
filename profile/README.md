## About The Project
<br />
Tanamify is a mobile app using machine learning to optimize crop selection based on local soil and climate data.
We understand the significant challenges farmers and communities face in assessing land suitability for farming, primarily due to limited knowledge of soil characteristics. This often leads to reliance on traditional planting methods, resulting in mismatches between soil types and the crops grown, ultimately lowering crop yields. ğŸŒ¾ Additionally, local climate conditions play a crucial role in impacting agricultural productivity.
